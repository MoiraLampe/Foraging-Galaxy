Welcome

Thank you for taking the time to participate in my experiment.

In this study, we want to investigate human behavior in a foraging game. In this game you will have to collect ressources in a galaxy with infinite planets.

The study consists of an instruction page, comprehension questions to make sure the task is well understood, a foraging game and a questionnaire. This experiment is going to take about 15-20 minutes. The game will be fast paced. Your full attention is needed for this period. Therefore, we would like to ask you to remove any distractions (e.g. phones) before starting to play the game.

At the end of the game, we will ask a couple of behavioral questions and demographic questions for statistical purposes. Therefore, we would like to point out that your identity will remain completely anonymous and any information provided will remain completely confidential.
